There Are Musicians & There Are Musicians (WIP)

28.07.2024

The first type...

...of musician is someone who goes from 0 to 1. When listening to a song by a first type musician you immediately hear that they were able to create a new sound. This does not need to be a new genre. A new rhythym or special sound do the trick.
Let me give you an example. When you listen to Rampa's & &ME's remix of the song "More Love" by Moderat (YouTube) you will hear this sound first appearing around the 1:40 mark. As far as I know, this is new (and I listen to a lot of music...). I do not even know what that sound is but it is exciting and drives the song forward.
